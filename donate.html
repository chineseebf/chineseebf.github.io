Who is E Bible Fellowship and why should I donate?

E Bible Fellowship is a non-profit christian organization that is not affiliated with any church. To learn more about us visit our About page. All of our efforts and resources go towards being obedient to Christ’s command to “feed My sheep,“ as found in John chapter 21. You can be assured your donation will be used wisely for the furtherance of God’s Word. For those who would like to financially support the sending out of the truth of the Word of God through E Bible Fellowship, listed below are the various ways you can donate online or you may donate by check.
