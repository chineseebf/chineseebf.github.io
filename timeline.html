Revelation 8:1 And when he had opened the seventh seal, there was silence in heaven about the space of half an hour.

There is rejoicing in heaven over one sinner that repents (has been saved by God and given a new heart), but because there was silence in heaven, that means there is no rejoicing for joy, which means there was no salvation.

Time Paths to 2033 A.D.

What is a Time Path?

A time path is taking a date found in the Bible, such as the birth of an individual, a major event, such as the worldwide flood (Genesis 7), or Jesus dying on the cross, and projecting that to a significant key date in the future to see how many years are between the two dates. Then that number can be broken down to see other numbers and their spiritual meanings, e.g., "40" in the Bible is the number for testing, "3" is the purpose of God, and so on.

Calendar Years vs Actual Years

When we add up the years from both calendar timelines below, when have to minus one year, because there is no year zero. These are what we call actual years. If we don't minus one, these are called calendar years.

As an example, let's use 2011 A.D. -- if we add 11,013 years to 2011, we get 13,024 as the number of Calendar Years.
So 13,024 is our Calendar year. Now we have to minus one, since there is no year zero. 13,024 - 1 = 13,023. So 13,023 is our actual year.

How is the calendar read?

When viewing this calendar of history, it may be difficult for some to understand what B.C and A.D means. B.C means "Before Christ", and A.D. means "Anno Domini" ("the year of the Lord"). The calendar the world uses now is the result of an attempt to have the calendar line up with the birth of Christ, although it can be shown that it is off by about 7 years, since Christ was born in 7 B.C. The year 2000 A.D was not 2000 years from Christ, 1994 was.

2011 A.D. marked the 13,023rd year of the earth, and on May 21, 2011, the Great Tribulation ended and the Day of Judgment began. The Biblical evidence is mounting that the earth will reach the age of 13,045 actual years, in 2033 A.D., when God will destroy this present universe and will create a new heavens and new earth.
